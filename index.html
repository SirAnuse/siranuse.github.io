Rustirealms 0.5.8.2 [CURRENT]
- Added Marble Seal functionality

-- The Wakizashi is currently partially functioning but it works nonetheless
- Knight shields have been buffed in PvP
-- Knight shields now do 1.25x damage in PvP

- Also the Klansman's favourite.

-- Regen rate was too damn slow in PvP fights, healers dominate way too much because of that

-- A reasonable nerf to the buff, would often heal 200+ HP for Paladins/Priests
- Nexus fountains now heal MP and can target multiple players
-- It was a bit annoying entering a fight without full MP
- Fixed small bug where MP Cost would show null next to it
-- Unnecessary client updates ez

Rustirealms 0.5.8.1
- Added Bottled Medusozoan functionality
- Added Orb of Aether functionality
- Added Enchantment Orb functionality
- Added functional skull wismod
- Tricksters can now function in PvP
-- Something of a scout class, you see where your decoys are
- Scepters have received a nerf in PvP
-- Scepter of Fulmination, Scepter of Devastation and Grotesque Scepter no longer target multiple players

Rustirealms 0.5.8

-- You still can't buy them, though, and your HP and MP pot amounts will be reset upon entering the arena.
- Fixed minor PvP bug where shots wouldn't go through players in PvP while you weren't in PvP
-- That was a mouthful
- Fixed Armour Piercing not being purple
-- This was because I moved the damage packet to the serverside, and client didn't like that
- Also fixed a bug where you couldn't join the same PvP arena twice
-- Wasn't flushing the dictionaries, oops
