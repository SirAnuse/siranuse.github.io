Version History
Rustirealms 0.5.8.1 [CURRENT]
- Added Bottled Medusozoan functionality
- Added Orb of Aether functionality
- Added Enchantment Orb functionality
- Scepters have received a nerf in PvP
-- Scepter of Fulmination, Scepter of Devastation and Grotesque Scepter no longer target multiple players
-- All scepters have had their damage values halved in PvP
- Priests have had their tome MP costs increased by 25 in PvP
-- I felt priests were too overpowered and could spam their tomes far too much
- Ritualist has been nerfed in PvP
-- Damage deflection has been halved

Rustirealms 0.5.8
- Added three more PvP maps
-- Ruthven's Arena, a 4p duo map.
-- Shaitan's Arena, a 3p FFA map.
-- Paradise Arena, a 4p FFA map.

- Reduced UT drop rates in the standard PvP chests
-- I felt that there wasn't much incentive to loot after you got the good stuff
-- New UT chests now spawn near the center of the map, with higher UT drop rates and to incentivise going to the center of the map
-- Mini-consumable chests spawn too, and drop HP Pots, Ghost Pirate Rums, and Speed Sprouts.
- With the introduction of mini-consumable chests, HP pots can be consumed during PvP
-- You still can't buy them, though, and your HP and MP pot amounts will be reset upon entering the arena.
- Fixed minor PvP bug where shots wouldn't go through players in PvP while you weren't in PvP
-- That was a mouthful
- Fixed Armour Piercing not being purple
-- This was because I moved the damage packet to the serverside, and client didn't like that
- Also fixed a bug where you couldn't join the same PvP arena twice
-- Wasn't flushing the dictionaries, oops
